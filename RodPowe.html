• Rod Powe

    (Solicitor Director)

    Rod Powe brings some 20 years experience as a family law specialist and advocate to Powe & White Family Lawyers

    Throughout his career Rod has been making a difference in the lives of his clients, many of whom are going through a difficult transition. It’s important for Rod that his clients get through the process with dignity and remain socially, economically and emotionally intact.

    Rod's experience extends not just to parents or adults within the family law system, but also as a experienced Independent Children's Lawyer, where he has represented children of all ages in matters before the Court. This experience has given Rod a unique perspective of the family law system, with the benefits of such experience offered to all of his clients.

    One of Rod's main goal as a family lawyer is to enhance the delivery of family law to a diverse client group in the most cost effective way.

    Rod ensures that client’s remain focussed on resolving matters through solution based thinking.
