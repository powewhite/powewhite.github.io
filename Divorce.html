Divorce & Separation

At any time in someone’s life, a separation or divorce is a major life changing event. Ensuring that you obtain expert legal advice at the earliest possibility can often mean that you can resolve any family law issues quickly and amicably.

It is important to remember that a divorce is a separate legal process from resolving parenting arrangements for children, or property settlement.

A divorce or separation does not finalise these other issues and time limits do apply to the resolution of property settlements.

Divorce officially ends a marriage once it has broken down.

To have a divorce order granted, a married couple must have been separated for at least one year prior to the time an application for divorce is filed and must satisfy the court that the relationship has broken down permanently.

Where there are children of the marriage under the age of eighteen years, the court must also be satisfied that arrangements for their future care are satisfactory.
