Private Agreements

At Powe & White Family Lawyers we understand the emotional and financial stress that a separation can have on clients. Our lawyers believe in trying to resolve matters as quickly and reasonably possible so as to minimise both the emotional and financial toll that some cases can have on clients.

In most cases, entering into private discussion with the other party or their lawyer can resolve matters by agreement.

The usual process involves an exchange in communication between the parties and/or correspondence between the lawyers.

At Powe & White Family Lawyers, our lawyers are highly trained in communicating effectively through both written and oral forms, therefore increasing the prospect of successfully resolving matters through private negotiation.

In some matters, it may be necessary for the parties to negotiate through mediation. This process involves bringing together each party and their lawyer, either in person at the 'round table' or over the phone.

Depending on how private negotiations are progressing, our lawyers will advise you on whether mediation or alternatively court proceedings may be necessary.
