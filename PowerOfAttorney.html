Power of Attorney

Power of Attorney and Enduring Guardianship are two legal documents which convey two distinct “powers” upon individuals.

Power of Attorney allows a person to allocate financial decision-making to another person in the event they are unable to make those decisions for themselves.

Enduring Guardianship allows a person to allocate medical decision-making to another person in the event they are unable to make those decisions for themselves.
