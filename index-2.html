    @skypename As a family lawyer Christopher White is dedicated to helping his clients overcome the social, legal and financial difficulties they face after their relationship has broken down. He works closely with clients to help reduce the stress and anxiety that they often face during a very difficult time in their lives. Through various means of dispute resolution, Christopher tries where possible to resolve matters without resorting to long, drawn-out legal proceedings. “I constantly remind both myself and my clients to redirect the focus away from minor issues the dispute at hand, and focus rather on the key issues and the long-term goals of all of the relevant parties. This often results in the best outcome for everyone concerned.” Christopher has acquired a range of experience working with clients from diverse backgrounds and he always seeks to ensure that clients are not burdened by complex legal concepts, instead explaining such concepts in simple and easily understood terms.
